What is WritersMob?

WritersMob is a place for writers to work on their creative writing skill by doing fun daily writing challenges.

Daily Challenge

Commit 5 minutes to write whatever is in your mind in response to the challenge. Go beyond 5 minutes if you're on a roll.

Read & Share your thoughts

Read what others wrote to the challenge, and provide positive and constructive feedbacks.

Track your progress & gain insights

Measure how many words you write daily. Also, see which words you use most frequently, what emotions your words convey and more.
